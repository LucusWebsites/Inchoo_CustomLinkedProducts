Inchoo_CustomLinkedProducts
===========================

Currently there are three types of product relations in Magento: Up-sells, Related Products, and Cross-sell Products. Recently while discussing client's requirement, we come to conclusion it would be best to link products in the same way, and then use our own relations independently of Magento's built in product relations. This Magento extension illustrates adding custom product relations as addition to existing Magento product relations.
